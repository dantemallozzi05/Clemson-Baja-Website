What is Baja SAE?

Baja SAE is a collegiate engineering competition involving student teams from across the nation collaborating to design, build, test, and race a offroad single-seater. Teams are assessed across several categories considering design, cost, agility, suspension, and torque. Through these examinations / competitions, members gain hands-on industry level skills with mechanical design, fabrication, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Who We Are

Clemson Baja SAE is a student-run engineering organization originally founded in 1978. For years, the team has represented Clemson on an international stage, carrying along a a tradition of creativity and engineering excellence. Members work together to design, manufacture, and test every aspect of our off-road vehicle. Our club fosters a community grounded in leadership, learning, and drive,
