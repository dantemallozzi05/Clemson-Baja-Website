Join Us

Build. Test. Race. Learn by doing with Clemson Baja SAE - the hand-on team where anyone can contribute!

No experience required. Just show up curious and with the desire to learn.

Frequently asked questions

What does the Baja team do?
Baja SAE is a collegiate engineering competition where we design, build, and race an off-road vehicle as a team.
Is it too late to join?
Not at all. People join all year, and if you’re motivated we’ll get you involved fast.
Do I need to be studying Engineering?
Nope. We need a variety of team members, including business, CS, Media, and more!
Do I need prior experience?
Also no. Most people start from zero. You’ll learn by building with the team.
