About

What is Baja SAE?

Baja SAE is a collegiate engineering competition involving student teams from across the nation collaborating to design, build, test, and race a offroad single-seater. Teams are assessed across several categories considering design, cost, agility, suspension, and torque.

Prove in comp that your car is durable, managable, and tinkerable while balancing the challenges of real-world engineering, component management, and collaboration.

Who We Are

Clemson Baja SAE is a student-run organization comprised of designers, builders, and racers from a variety of fields.
